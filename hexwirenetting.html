HEX WIRE NETTING

It is used as wire mesh fabrics for light reinforcement in splinter proof glass and cement concrete, plastering and laying of roads, etc.
Hex netting is an economical product used to secure other materials in place, such as insulation to roofs. Hex netting is ideal for lawn and garden and other home projects.


Various applications that hex netting can be used for:

  • Wire mesh fencing
  • Flood control hex netting
  • Construction reinforcement hex netting
  • General purpose wire netting
  • Wire mesh window guards
  • Wire mesh tree guards
  • Trellis for climbing plants and flowers
  • Framework for sculpture and float decorations
