INDUSTRIAL HEAVY SCREEN

Mahaveera Industries is one of the leading manufacturer of industrial wire screen meshes providing high tensile abrasion resistant screens.

The strength and free screening area advantages of woven wire screens over other screening media make them suitable for many industrial sifting and separating applications. As well as supplying to the mine and quarry industry, the screens are used in many other areas including precious metals, ore grading, water treatment, coal preparation, foundries, waste management, agriculture and food processing.



About us

Mahaveera Industries is one of the largest manufacturers and suppliers of Industrial Wire Cloth and Screen Meshes in India. The Company has re-organised and updated its manufacturing capacity to include the most modern methods and equipment across the entire range of woven wire specifications.
