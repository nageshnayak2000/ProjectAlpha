ABOUT US

Mahaveera Industries is one of the largest manufacturers and suppliers of industrialwire cloth and screen meshes in India. The Company has a long history in the industry and in recent years has re-organised and updated its manufacturing capacity to include the most modern methods and equipment across the entire range of woven wire specifications Industrial Wire Cloth.

With more than 10 years experience in weaving technology, Mahaveera Industries has ability to manufacture all types of wire mesh from the finest micronic filter cloths to the heaviest precrimped screens for the mining and quarrying industry. This depth of experience is important, but so too is planning for the future. A continuous program of development and experimentation is operated, involving new types of weave, methods and material to ensure that the highest manufacturing standard and product performance is achieved.
